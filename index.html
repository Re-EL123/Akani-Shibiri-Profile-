About Me

I grew up in love with philosophy and science, which led me to a fascination with electronics. In grade five, I discovered my mother's computer and learned CMD & Batch, writing scripts to create simple games. By grade nine, I had honed my skills in graphics design using Paint and Microsoft Office. In grade ten, I delved into ethical hacking, sparking my desire to build my own software. Using an Android phone, I began editing Android apps, which taught me XML schemas and Java.

From grade eleven, I actively pursued Java, enrolling in Rosebank College for a diploma in software development. Financial struggles limited me to five modules, but my passion never waned. From 2019 to 2023, I worked in retail while freelancing as a graphic designer and refining my programming skills. Today, I code in HTML, JavaScript, CSS, PHP, SQL, and C#.
